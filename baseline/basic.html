Investment Philosophy

Today’s financial institutions focus too much on gathering assets from retail investors with slogans like:​​ “Open and account today and qualify to get 1000 free trades for 6 months”. At ​iPlanner we feel that the industry is leaning too heavily in converting every investor out there to a trader. Financial literacy and know how including successful trading, comes at a price. It takes time and effort to navigate through the clutter of financial media and the coveted “1000 free trades” for a new investor that is just getting started is the equivalent of giving a toddler a gun and 1000 bullets. There are savvy traders out there that are able to consistently earn above-average returns no matter what the market conditions might be. They could certainly benefit from 1000 free trades. However the moms and pops, the students, the young professionals who don’t have the luxury of following the markets every day trying to exploit the opportunities out there (and there are plenty) are better off starting to invest in an efficient way.

We are proponents of passive index investing using highly liquid, low fee ETFs tracking various asset classes like equities, bonds, cash instruments and understanding how they perform over time by placing a high emphasis on diversification and long-run returns. We aim to create technology that will enable investors to build a goal-based investment strategy in a fast and transparent way. Today’s investment advice industry is not very transparent and that is how they have been able to charge high fees for decades and very often underperforming their own benchmarks. Traditional fees can range between 1-2.5%. in the long run, the excess fees paid to can lead to underperformance for a portfolio tracking the same strategy. iPlanner advocates that for the retail investors who want to invest and not take 50 - 100 shots day-trading, there is a better way! Below is a detailed overview of our approach. We encourage you to read through it as it will provide some useful insight.
